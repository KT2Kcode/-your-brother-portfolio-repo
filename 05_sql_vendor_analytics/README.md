### 05_sql_vendor_analytics
* **Goal**: Designed and queried a relational database system tracking corporate vendor profiles, billing addresses, and invoice payment lifecycles.
* **Projects Included**:
  * **Vendor Address Information**: Designed schema to hold core vendor identities, contact data, and multi-location mailing addresses while enforcing data integrity constraints.
  * **Invoice Payment SQL**: Built transactional tables tracking historical invoices, due dates, payment statuses, and remaining balances.
  * **Vendor Payments Analysis**: Authored complex analytical queries utilizing `INNER JOIN`, `GROUP BY`, and mathematical aggregations to calculate total spending, average days-to-pay, and outstanding liabilities per vendor.
* **Key Skills Used**: Relational Database Design, Primary & Foreign Keys, Table Normalization, Data Aggregation (`SUM`, `COUNT`), and Multi-Table Join optimization.
